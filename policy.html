Social Media Policy

At Sagittarius Muse, we strive to create an inspiring community built on the principles of mutual respect, ethics, and aesthetic integrity.

  • RESPECT: Any forms of aggression or discrimination will be removed immediately.
  • INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY: We value original creative work and expect the same from our readers.
  • AESTHETIC DIALOGUE: We welcome constructive criticism that contributes to collective inspiration.

Sagittarius Muse is a territory of safety, high style, and intellectual pursuit.
